2) What is generally required or strongly recommended
A. Data protection (GDPR baseline)
- Privacy notice covering data categories, lawful basis, retention, and user rights.
- Process for data subject rights requests (access/deletion/correction).
- Processor documentation for providers handling user data.

B. Processing records
- Maintain internal records of processing activities where applicable.

C. Security and incident readiness
- Technical and organizational security controls.
- Incident response process, including breach notification workflow where required.

D. Terms and policy controls
- Terms of Service, Acceptable Use, and Safety policy must clearly define prohibited behavior.
- Enforcement and suspension policy should be explicit.

E. Consumer/billing transparency
- Clear subscription pricing, renewal behavior, cancellation, and refund handling.
- Merchant-of-record/payment flow details should be unambiguous.

F. 18+ and sensitive-content boundaries
- Age gating and strict minor-related prohibitions should remain explicit.
- Adult topics mode must not bypass legal or policy restrictions.
